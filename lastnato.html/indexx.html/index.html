How to cook
RICE?

WONDERING HOW TO COOK RICE?

Step 1

Prepare rice and rice cooker

Step 2

Put the rice in the rice cooker

Step 3

Add water and rinse the rice two times.

Step 4

Using your ring finger, measure the level of rice and add water, the water level should be the same level with the level of rice in the rice cooker

Step 5

After that, turn on heat and wait until it's cooked

Cooked rice

This is what what it should look like after following the above steps.
